Stories have the power to impact, inspire, and transform us. They can make us laugh, make us cry, give us escape to worlds beyond our imagination.

But the way we tell stories has been largely stagnant. In an age where I can have a conversation with my computer, an e-book is still a book...just rendered on a screen.

Boring.

Pixel & Prose aims to bring technological sophistication and innovation to the stories we love the most. I believe in experiences that put us in the story and give us agency, without compromising emotional depth.

Stories that adapt.

Stories that transform.

Because stories were never meant to sit quietly on the page.

But be careful—not everything that stays alive stays tame.
