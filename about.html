My name is Tori Jones

And I believe there's a better way to tell stories.

As a little girl, I was always hungry for ways to feel closer to the stories I loved—Twilight, Pretty Little Liars, Charmed, Percy Jackson and the Lightning Thief, Gossip Girl. These were the stories that let me escape from the day to day and feel what it was like to have extraordinary things happen, even for a very ordinary girl in the Chicago suburbs.

Now, surrounded by prequels, sequels, and remakes, I am passionate about finding ways to combine sophisticated storylines with innovative ways of storytelling. Stories weren’t meant to only sit on a page, and we weren’t meant to idle quietly while the narrative happens to someone else.

The lines were always meant to be blurred.

Welcome to Pixel & Prose.

-Tori
